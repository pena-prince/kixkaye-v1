About KixKaye Singaporean Hawker Food House

KixKaye Singaporean Hawker Food House is the pioneer restaurant in Naga City that offers Singaporean-Malaysian fusion of authentic dishes and flavours. To its extent, KixKaye also satisfies Indian and Chinese plates as it offers wide array of dishes indigenous to its kind.
